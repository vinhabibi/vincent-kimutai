04 Coffee Sales Analysis
Dashboard → GitHub →

E-commerce coffee shop sales analysis on 3K+ transactions ($1.1M revenue) — revenue trends by product, time-of-day patterns, seasonal peaks, and AOV breakdown across a full year using Power BI and DAX.

💡 What I Learnt
Built DAX measures using SUMMARIZE & TOPN to identify peak hour (10 AM). Learned cross-filtering — one month slicer driving all 6 visuals simultaneously. Discovered that a near-even 33/34/34% morning/afternoon/night split means demand is 24/7, not just morning-heavy as assumed.
📊 Key Numbers
$1.1M revenue · 3K+ transactions · $31.65 avg order · Latte & Cappuccino = 45% of revenue · March peaks at 15.9K sales · July dips to 6.4K · Monday AOV highest at $31.92
🛠 Skills Gained
Power BI dashboard design, DAX calculated measures, CSV data modelling, business analytics storytelling, revenue vs transaction volume comparison, and seasonal trend identification for campaign planning.
